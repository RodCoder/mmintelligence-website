01 — Identity
Wordmark

The MM Intelligence wordmark is typeset entirely in Inter, with differentiated weight between "MM" (500) and "INTELLIGENCE" (300). Wide tracking creates institutional distance. No logomark or icon — the word is the mark.

Usage notes

Use the gold variant on dark backgrounds — it is the primary expression. The muted white-35 variant appears in footers and secondary contexts. Never use full white (#FFFFFF) for the wordmark. Never alter the weight split between MM and INTELLIGENCE.

The space between "MM" and "INTELLIGENCE" is a literal non-breaking space character — preserve it in all implementations.

Motion

Motion is restrained and purposeful. Two systems: scroll-reveal for content entrance, scroll-position for the Hero animation. No decorative or looping animations beyond the scroll indicator.

Scroll reveal — entrance
opacity 0 → 1 + translateY(28px → 0)
Duration: 0.75s · Easing: cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94) · Trigger: Intersection Observer (threshold 0.15)
Stagger delays
100ms · 200ms · 300ms · 400ms · 500ms · 600ms
Applied via data-reveal-delay attribute. Use sequentially on grouped elements (cards, steps).

90 PNG frames · scroll-position driven
Container: 220vh. Sticky section: 100vh. Frame index = floor(progress × 90). All frames preloaded on mount.

translateY(scrolled × 0.08)

04 — Usage
Voice & Tone

The MM Intelligence voice is precise, institutional, and understated. We speak in full sentences, avoid superlatives, and let the quality of work speak without promotional language.

Use
Technical analysis to support structured evidencing and defensible narratives.
Avoid
Industry-leading cutting-edge blockchain forensics solutions.
Use
We keep biographies brief and avoid operational detail.
Avoid
Meet our amazing team of crypto experts!
Use
Operational intelligence for digital wealth.
Avoid
The #1 platform for digital asset intelligence!
Use
Start a Confidential Discussion.
Avoid
Get Started Free! Book a Demo!
Tone principles

Precision over volume

Fewer words, precisely chosen. Every sentence earns its place.

Institutional confidence

No hedging, no hype. State what is done, not how impressive it is.

Discretion as default

Client confidentiality is a product feature, not just a legal obligation. Language reflects this.

British spelling

Use evidencing, not evidencing; organisation not organization; favour not favor.

04 — Usage
What to Avoid

Hard rules. These protect the brand's institutional character and prevent visual dilution.

✓ Do

Use Cormorant Garamond at weight 300 (light) for all display and headline use. The lightness creates the institutional, editorial quality.

✗ Don't

Use Cormorant Garamond at weight 400 or 500 for headlines. Never bold the display font.

✓ Do

Use gold (#C9A84C) exclusively for: the wordmark, section labels, hover states, scroll indicator, and active nav links.

✗ Don't

Use any other accent colour. Never use blue, green, red, or any saturated colour in any UI element.

✓ Do

Use border-radius of 0px (panels, cards) or 1px (buttons, inputs) exclusively.

✗ Don't

Use rounded corners (4px, 8px, 50% pill) on any element. They conflict with the architectural, precise character of the brand.

✓ Do

Use generous vertical padding (96–128px per section). Whitespace communicates restraint and confidence.

✗ Don't

Compress sections to fit more content. If content doesn't fit, reduce content — not padding.

✓ Do

Open all external links (mmlaw.global) in a new tab with rel="noopener noreferrer".

✗ Don't

Add drop shadows, box-shadows (beyond blur in glass-panel), gradients on text, or any skeuomorphic effects.
